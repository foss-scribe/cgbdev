## Bio

I’m Chris, a versatile tech professional who's worked for more than 20 years as a technical writer and web developer. I was an early adopter of docs-as-code, combining my skills in writing and developing websites to create scalable docs long before the practice was mainstream. 

I have broad industry participation, including manufacturing, banking and financial services, engineering services, software development, sports tech, analytics and publishing. I have worked for Sportsbet, Canva, Specialized, Equifax and 3/4 of Australia's 'big four' banks.

As of 2025, technical writing's been transformed by Artificial Intelligence, bringing an abrupt end to my career at Canva. After much soul-searching, I've pivoted, adopting AI like I did doc-as-code 12 years ago. I now help companies adapt their documentation and development environments to using Artificial Intelligence.

Originally from Wales in the UK, I grew up and was educated in Australia. I received a Bachelors and Masters Degree from the University of Melbourne. An avid traveller in my 20s, I lived and worked in Bristol, England for 3 years, but returned to Australia to settle down. Today, I live in Melbourne with my wife and three children.
